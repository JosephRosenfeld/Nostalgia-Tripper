What is the Nostalgia Tripper

(Besides an awesome name of course)

The Nostalgia Tripper is a free to use, cloud based tool that scans publicly available databases for content that it thinks you'll find nostalgic.

Once you’re signed up, you’ll receive a daily email with either an image, video, or message that will let your brain take a glimpse into the past and experience a rush of feel good endorphins. This is what we in the biz like to call, a Nostalgia Trip.

The Benefits of Nostalgia

Reduced Stress

Nostalgia has many pychological impacts, but the biggest one is the simple fact that it feels good! More frequent occurances of this emotion have been found to correlate with greater overall feelings of happiness and a reduction in stress.

Increased Empathy

Nostalgia has been hypothesized to be one of the most unifying emotions you can experience. It leads to increased feelings of interconectedness and a big boost to your empathy!

Greater Resilience

Nostalgia has been found to play a huge role in helping the individual weather signficant life changes or becoming comfortable with the idea of a new state in life, such as transitioning into adulthood or contemplating death.
